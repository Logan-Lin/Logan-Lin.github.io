Hi, my name is Yan Lin,
A CS Researcher

My research interests lies in Spatial-temporal Data Mining, Trajectory Mining, Representation Learning, and Pre-training.

I received the B.S. degree in computer science from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2019, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science in 2024.
I am currently a Post-Doc. in the DAISY group, Aalborg University.
